# Choozle Plans

Choozle offers subscription-based pricing plans for self-service programmatic advertising, starting at $99/month. Plans are designed for agencies, brands, and media buyers of all sizes.

## Self-Service Plan

- **Starting Price:** $99/month (flat rate)
- Unlimited advertiser accounts under one organization
- Access to 60+ premium third-party data providers
- Campaign management across Display, CTV, Video, Audio, Native, DOOH, Search, and Social
- Real-time bidding via The Trade Desk, Amazon Ads, and Walmart Connect
- AI-powered optimization across ten performance dimensions
- Reporting API access (HMAC-SHA256 authentication)
- Personal support and online learning resources
- Role-based user access and permission management
- Smart Container Tag for pixel deployment
- Google Analytics integration
- Access to 10,000+ private marketplace deals

## Managed Services (Add-On)

- 10% management fee on campaign spend
- Dedicated digital media expert support
- Managed execution across all supported channels

## Account Balance Requirements

- Prepaid account balance model
- Minimum recommended balance: $500
- Auto-refill available when balance drops to preset minimum
- Campaigns suspended if balance is insufficient for 24+ hours
- Media fees include: account balance, media costs, data targeting costs, ad serving costs, third-party tool costs, and optimization fees

## Enterprise / Custom Plans

- Custom pricing available for larger organizations
- Contact Choozle sales for tailored packages

## Notes

- All fees billed in U.S. dollars
- No credit card trial; self-service onboarding with account balance deposit
- LiveRamp integration for first-party data onboarding available
